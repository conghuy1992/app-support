About Solitaire: Timeless Card

Solitaire: Timeless Card brings the timeless Klondike solitaire experience to your mobile device with modern visuals and intuitive controls.

Enjoy infinite puzzles that never feel repetitive, carefully balanced across Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty levels. Whether you want to relax or challenge your mind, there is always a perfect game waiting for you.

The game works fully offline, allowing you to play anytime, anywhere. Gameplay recording helps you review your sessions, while Light Mode and Dark Mode ensure a comfortable experience day or night.

FAQ

Q: Is Solitaire: Timeless Card free to play?
A: Yes. The game is free to play and does not require an account.

Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes. The game works fully offline.

Q: What solitaire rules are used?
A: The game follows classic Klondike solitaire rules.

Q: Are there difficulty levels?
A: Yes. Easy, Medium, and Hard levels are available.

Q: Does the game have unlimited puzzles?
A: Yes. Solitaire: Timeless Card offers infinite gameplay.

Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes. Progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Q: Is Dark Mode supported?
A: Yes. Both Light Mode and Dark Mode are supported.
