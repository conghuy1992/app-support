Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Number Maze: Zip ("the App") handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency, minimal data collection, and protecting user data.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: Number Maze: Zip stores all game-related data locally on your device. This includes puzzle progress, completed levels, difficulty settings (Easy, Medium, Expert), gameplay history, and appearance preferences such as light or dark mode. This allows the App to function fully offline.

No personal information: The App does not request, collect, or require personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

2. Analytics and Advertising

Number Maze: Zip may use third-party analytics or advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to improve the App experience and support development through advertisements. These services may collect non-personal information, including device type, operating system version, or anonymous advertising identifiers.

We do not directly access, store, or control data collected by third-party providers. You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising through your device’s system settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit personal or gameplay data to any external server. If future versions introduce online features such as cloud saves or leaderboards, your explicit consent will be requested in advance.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect data, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. Use of the App is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

Number Maze: Zip is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such data is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party services such as advertising networks, analytics tools, or external links. This Privacy Policy applies only to Number Maze: Zip and does not cover third-party services. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.
