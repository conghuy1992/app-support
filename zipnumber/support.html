About Number Maze: Zip

Number Maze: Zip is a minimalist logic puzzle game with a clear objective: draw a single uninterrupted line starting from number 1, passing through all numbers in order, and covering every cell in the grid.

Each puzzle requires careful planning and spatial reasoning. You cannot lift your finger or cross the path, making every move meaningful and satisfying. The game is easy to learn but becomes increasingly challenging as the grid grows more complex.

Number Maze: Zip features infinite puzzle generation, full offline play, and multiple difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Expert. Your gameplay history is recorded so you can review progress, improve strategies, and challenge yourself further. Light and dark mode support ensures comfortable play anytime.

FAQ

Q: Is Number Maze: Zip free to play?
A: Yes. The game is free to play and does not require an account.

Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes. Number Maze: Zip works fully offline. No internet connection is required.

Q: How do I play?
A: Draw one continuous line starting from number 1, connecting all numbers in order while covering every cell.

Q: Are there difficulty levels?
A: Yes. The game includes Easy, Medium, and Expert levels.

Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes. Puzzle history and progress are stored locally on your device.

Q: Does the game support dark mode?
A: Yes. You can switch between light and dark modes in the settings.
