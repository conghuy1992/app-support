Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application ColorLink: Color Connect ("the App") handles user information. We value your privacy and aim to keep data usage minimal, transparent, and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: ColorLink: Color Connect stores game-related data such as puzzle progress, completed levels, difficulty selection, gameplay recordings, and user preferences locally on your device. This allows the App to be fully playable offline.

No personal information: The App does not request or collect personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, or precise location information.

2. Analytics and Advertising

ColorLink: Color Connect may use third-party analytics or advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to improve gameplay experience and support development through advertisements.

These third-party services may collect non-personal information, including device type, operating system version, general usage statistics, and anonymous advertising identifiers. We do not directly access, store, or control data collected by these providers.

You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising through your device’s system settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. The App does not upload personal or gameplay data to external servers.

If future versions introduce online features such as cloud saves, leaderboards, or online challenges, users will be clearly informed and consent will be requested where required by law.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. Use of the App is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

ColorLink: Color Connect is suitable for players of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If any personal data from a child is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics tools. This Privacy Policy applies only to ColorLink: Color Connect and does not cover third-party services.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those third-party providers independently.

6. Policy Updates and Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Information may be disclosed if required by applicable laws or legal authorities.
