About ColorLink: Color Connect

ColorLink: Color Connect is a visual logic puzzle game where your goal is to connect all matching color pairs to fill the entire grid.

Each level starts with colored dots placed on the board. Your task is to draw smooth paths between matching colors so that every color is connected correctly.

Paths must follow clear rules: lines cannot cross, there are no branches or T-junctions, paths cannot loop back or touch, and every square must be filled. Each puzzle has exactly one valid solution.

ColorLink is designed to be relaxing, intuitive, and deeply satisfying. With clean visuals and simple rules, the challenge comes from careful planning and pattern recognition.

FAQ

Q: Is ColorLink free to play?
A: Yes. ColorLink is free to play and does not require an account.

Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes. All puzzles are fully playable offline.

Q: Are there different difficulty levels?
A: Yes. ColorLink includes Beginner, Medium, and Expert levels.

Q: Does the game save my progress?
A: Yes. Progress and settings are saved locally on your device.

A: Yes. The game supports both light and dark modes.
