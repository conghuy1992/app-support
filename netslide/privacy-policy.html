Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Net Slide - Netwalk ("the App") handles and protects user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is used.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: Net Slide - Netwalk stores your puzzle progress, difficulty selection, play history, and interface preferences (such as light or dark mode) locally on your device. This allows offline play and lets you continue anytime.

No personal data collection: The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, or photos during normal gameplay.

2. Analytics and Advertising

The App may use third-party services (for example, Google AdMob or analytics providers) to help improve the experience and display advertisements. These services may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, general interaction data, or advertising identifiers.

This data is not personally identifiable to us. You can manage ad personalization through your device privacy settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

All gameplay data and settings remain stored on your device. The App does not upload your puzzle solutions or history to external servers.

If future updates introduce online features such as cloud save, leaderboards, or accounts, we will request your permission before any information is transmitted.

While we take reasonable steps to protect information, no method of storage is completely secure. Use of the App is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

Net Slide - Netwalk is designed for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party SDKs for advertising or analytics. This Privacy Policy applies only to Net Slide - Netwalk. Please review the privacy policies of those providers for details about how they manage data.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Significant updates may also be communicated inside the App.
