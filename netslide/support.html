About Net Slide - Netwalk

Net Slide - Netwalk is a relaxing yet challenging logic puzzle. Use arrows to move entire rows or columns and rearrange tiles until all computers are connected in a single network.

Every square must be used, and connections cannot pass through red barriers. With multiple difficulty levels from Easy to Hard, the game grows with your skills.

Enjoy offline play, track your puzzle history, and switch between light and dark themes for comfortable gameplay anytime, anywhere.
