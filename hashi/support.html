About Hashi ‚Äì Bridges Puzzle

Hashi ‚Äì Bridges Puzzle is a clean and relaxing logic puzzle inspired by the classic Japanese Hashiwokakero game. Each island contains a number indicating how many bridges must connect to it. Your goal is to link all islands into one connected network.

Bridges can only be placed horizontally or vertically. They cannot cross each other, and no more than two bridges can connect the same pair of islands. The challenge comes from satisfying all clues while keeping the entire grid fully connected.

The game supports Easy, Medium, and Hard levels, infinite puzzle generation, offline play, and gameplay statistics tracking.
