Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Hashi – Bridges Puzzle ("the App") handles user information. We value your privacy and are committed to keeping data collection minimal, transparent, and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Local gameplay data only: Hashi – Bridges Puzzle stores game-related information such as puzzle progress, selected difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard), gameplay statistics, and preferences locally on your device. This enables full offline functionality.

No personal information required: The App does not request or collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

2. Analytics and Advertising

Hashi – Bridges Puzzle may use third-party analytics or advertising services (for example, Google AdMob) to improve the user experience and support the App through advertisements. These services may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system version, and anonymous advertising identifiers.

We do not directly access, store, or control data collected by third-party providers. You may manage or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

All puzzle progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit personal or gameplay data to external servers.

If future updates introduce optional features such as cloud backup, online leaderboards, or user accounts, your explicit consent will be requested before any data collection.

While reasonable safeguards are implemented, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

4. Children’s Privacy

Hashi – Bridges Puzzle is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such data is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics tools. This Privacy Policy applies only to Hashi – Bridges Puzzle and does not govern third-party services. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those providers.
