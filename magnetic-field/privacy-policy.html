Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Magnetic Field Nova (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to operate without collecting personal data. We do not require account creation, and we do not access your contacts, photos, calendar, or files.

  • Magnetometer (required): We read the device’s magnetometer to measure magnetic field strength in real time. Raw sensor values are processed on-device only.
  • Notes (optional): Any note you enter for a snapshot/session is stored locally with that entry.

2. How We Use Data

  • Show live magnetic field values and charts.
  • Save snapshots (single readings) or session recordings (time series) to your on-device history.
  • Improve reading stability via on-device filtering and calibration guidance.

3. Data Storage & Retention

All measurements, notes, and (if provided) locations are stored locally on your device. You can delete entries from History at any time or uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.

4. Analytics & Advertising

The App currently does not send your personal data to our servers. If we integrate privacy-friendly analytics or advertising (e.g., Google AdMob) in the future, such services may collect limited, non-personal information (e.g., device type, ad interactions, anonymous identifiers) to operate and improve their features. We will update this Policy and provide in-app notice if material changes occur.

5. Sensor & Permission Details

  • Magnetometer: Used solely to read magnetic field values. No raw data is transmitted off-device.
  • Location (optional): Used only when you choose to tag a log with your position. You can deny permission and continue using the App.

6. Calibration

The App may guide you to rotate your device so the operating system can refine sensor calibration. This process occurs on-device and does not transmit data to us.

7. Data Security

We use the operating system’s security features and store data locally. While no method is 100% secure, we apply reasonable measures to protect your data on-device.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date. Material changes may also be communicated in-app.
