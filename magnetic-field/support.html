Q: Why does the app need sensor permission?
A: Magnetic Field Nova uses the built-in magnetometer sensor. No other permissions are required, and no personal data is collected.

Q: What units are measurements displayed in?
A: The app shows results in microtesla (¬µT), the standard unit for magnetic field strength.

Q: What is the difference between a snapshot and a session record?
A: A snapshot saves a single measurement point with timestamp and optional note. A session record saves a continuous stream of measurements for later review.

Q: Does Magnetic Field Nova work offline?
A: Yes! All measurements and history storage work entirely offline.

Q: Can I export my measurement history?
A: Currently, data is stored locally for review within the app. Future updates may add export options.
