Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the mobile application Lucky Spin Quest.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The app does not require login, does not access your contacts, location, microphone, or photos. All custom wheel data and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or stored on any server.

2. Analytics and Advertising

Lucky Spin Quest may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect non-personal information (such as device type, ad interactions, or anonymous identifiers) to personalize ads. We do not control these services’ data practices. Please review their privacy policies for more details.

3. Data Security

Since the app does not collect personal data, your privacy is respected by default. Data such as your custom wheels and app preferences are saved locally and protected by your device’s security system.

4. Children's Privacy

Lucky Spin Quest is intended for general use and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe your child has submitted personal data, please contact us so we can remove the data promptly.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Please review it periodically.
