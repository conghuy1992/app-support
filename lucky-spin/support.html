Q: I lost my custom wheels. Can I recover them?
A: Custom wheels are saved locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, your wheels will be lost. We recommend backing up your data manually if needed.

Q: Can I use Lucky Spin Quest offline?
A: Yes! Once downloaded, the app works fully offline. No internet is required to spin or manage your wheels.

Q: Are there ads in the app?
A: Lucky Spin Quest is free to use and includes optional ads to support development. We aim to keep the ad experience minimal and non-intrusive.
