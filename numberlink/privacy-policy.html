Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Numberlink: ConnectFlow ("the App") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping our practices simple, transparent, and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: Numberlink: ConnectFlow stores your gameplay progress, difficulty level (Beginner, Medium, Advanced, Expert), and completed puzzles only on your device. This information allows you to continue playing and track your history offline.

No personal data collection: The App does not require user registration and does not collect your name, email, photos, contacts, microphone input, or precise location for normal gameplay.

2. Analytics and Advertising

We may integrate third-party analytics and advertising services such as Google AdMob to understand usage trends and serve ads. These providers may collect non-personal data like device model, OS version, or anonymous identifiers. We do not control or access their collected data directly; please review their respective privacy policies for details.

You may manage or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or through ad network preferences.

3. Data Storage and Security

All progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit personal or gameplay data to our servers. If cloud saving or synchronization features are added in the future, users will be notified and asked for consent before any data transfer.

While we take reasonable measures to secure data stored on your device, no digital system is completely secure. Use is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

Numberlink: ConnectFlow is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If any such information is discovered, please contact us to request deletion.

The App may include links or integrations to third-party services (for example, ad providers, analytics tools, or support links). This Privacy Policy applies only to Numberlink: ConnectFlow, not to external sites or services. Please refer to those third parties’ privacy policies for their data practices.
