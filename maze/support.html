About Maze Solver: One Path

Maze Solver: One Path is a clean and focused puzzle game built around a simple but challenging rule: each maze has only one valid solution.

Players move in four directions â€” up, down, left, and right. Once a direction is chosen, the player continues moving until blocked by a wall. This creates a unique puzzle experience that rewards planning, logical thinking, and foresight rather than trial and error.

The game works fully offline and includes multiple difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert. You can also customize the player icon theme and review completed boards through recorded solutions.

FAQ

Q: Is Maze Solver: One Path free to play?
A: Yes. The game is free to play and does not require an account.

Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: No. The game works completely offline. Progress is saved locally on your device.

Q: How does movement work?
A: Choose a direction and the player will continue moving until hitting a wall. Each level has only one correct path to the goal.

Q: Are there different difficulty levels?
A: Yes. The game includes Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert levels.

Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes. Completed levels and solved boards are stored locally.

Q: Can I customize the player icon?
A: Yes. You can select different player icon themes in the settings.
