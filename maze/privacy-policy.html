Last updated: October 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Maze Solver: One Path ("the App") handles user information. We value your privacy and are committed to minimizing data collection, ensuring transparency, and protecting user data.

Local data only: Maze Solver: One Path stores game-related data such as level progress, completed puzzles, difficulty selection, player icon themes, and gameplay recordings locally on your device. This allows the App to function fully offline.

No personal information: The App does not require you to provide personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

All gameplay data and preferences are stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit personal or gameplay data to any server. If future versions introduce online features such as cloud saves or leaderboards, your explicit consent will be requested in advance.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect data, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. Use of the App is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

Maze Solver: One Path is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such data is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

The App may include third-party services such as advertising networks, analytics tools, or external links. This Privacy Policy applies only to Maze Solver: One Path and does not cover third-party services. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.
