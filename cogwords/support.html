About Cogs Words: Letter Gears

Cogs Words: Letter Gears is a creative word puzzle game where each move rotates a 2x2 block of letters clockwise. Your goal is to align the gears to form correct words based on the category hint.

Every rotation affects multiple letters, so careful planning and logical thinking are essential. The game blends vocabulary skills with spatial reasoning, creating a satisfying brain-training experience.

Features include Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty levels, Infinite Puzzle mode, offline play, and game completion record tracking.

FAQ

Q: Is Cogs Words: Letter Gears free to play?
A: Yes. The game is free to play and does not require an account.

Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes. All puzzles can be played offline. Progress and records are stored locally on your device.

Q: How do I play?
A: Tap the center of a 2x2 block to rotate it clockwise. Align the letters to form correct words based on the category hint.

Q: Are there unlimited puzzles?
A: Yes. Infinite Mode provides endless word puzzle challenges.

Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes. Difficulty settings, completed levels, and records are saved locally on your device.
