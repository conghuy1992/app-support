Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how the mobile application SoundLytix handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

SoundLytix does not collect or store any personal information. The app does not require a login, and it does not access your contacts, location, photos, or files.

The microphone is used solely to measure real-time sound levels when the app is active and recording. No audio is stored, transmitted, or shared with any server.

2. Analytics and Advertising

SoundLytix may display ads using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect non-personal information (like device type, ad interactions, or anonymous identifiers) to personalize advertising. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more details.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. The app does not store any data externally, and all usage remains private to your device and protected by its security mechanisms.

4. Microphone Permission

The microphone is only accessed when measuring sound levels. The app does not record, save, or share audio. Microphone usage is clearly indicated, and permission is requested explicitly from the user.

5. Children's Privacy

SoundLytix is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided any personal data, please contact us so we can address the issue.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. We will post any changes on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Please review the policy periodically.
