Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Skyscraper Logic ("the App") handles and protects user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how data is used.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: Skyscraper Logic stores puzzle progress, difficulty settings, theme preferences, gameplay recordings, and statistics locally on your device. This enables offline play and allows you to resume your games anytime.

No personal data collection: The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location during normal gameplay.

2. Analytics and Advertising

The App may use third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics providers to improve the experience and support free gameplay. These services may collect non-personal information including device type, operating system version, general interaction data, and advertising identifiers.

This information is used only for analytics and advertisement purposes and is not personally identifiable to us. You can manage advertising preferences through your device privacy settings.

3. Offline Gameplay

Skyscraper Logic is fully playable offline. Puzzle generation, progress tracking, and statistics do not require an internet connection.

The App does not upload puzzle solutions, recordings, or gameplay history to external servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

All gameplay data and settings remain stored locally on your device. We do not operate user accounts or cloud storage features at this time.

If future updates introduce optional online features such as cloud save or leaderboards, we will request your consent before transmitting any information.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect information, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Skyscraper Logic is designed for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate third-party SDKs for advertising or analytics. This Privacy Policy applies only to Skyscraper Logic. Please review the privacy policies of those providers for details about how they manage data.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Significant changes may also be communicated within the App.
