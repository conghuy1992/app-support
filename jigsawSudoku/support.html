About Jigsaw Sudoku Puzzle

Jigsaw Sudoku Puzzle gives a creative twist to traditional Sudoku. The goal is familiar: every row, column, and region must contain the numbers 1-9 exactly once. The difference? Each of the nine regions is an irregular jigsaw shape, so no two puzzles feel the same.

Choose your challenge with five modes: Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, or a surprise Shuffle. Your progress and completed boards are stored locally so you can pick up where you left off at any time.
