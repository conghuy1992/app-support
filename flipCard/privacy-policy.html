Last updated: August 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the mobile app Nova Match (Flip Cards).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Nova Match does not require login, does not track your location, and does not access personal data such as contacts, photos, or microphone. Game data like high scores or settings are stored locally on your device only.

2. Analytics and Advertising

Nova Match may display ads using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect non-personal information (like device type, interaction behavior, or advertising ID) to provide better ad experiences. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

3. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, your privacy is safeguarded. All game data remains on your device, protected by your system’s own security measures. We use only trusted SDKs that follow industry-standard practices.

4. Children’s Privacy

This game is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has submitted personal data to us, please reach out so we can assist appropriately.
