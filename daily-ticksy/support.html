📄 FAQ

Q: Can I use Daily Ticksy offline?
A: Yes! Daily Ticksy works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

Q: Is Daily Ticksy free to use?
A: Yes. The app is free with optional ads to support development.

Q: Can I group tasks by day or view past task lists?
A: Yes. Tasks are automatically grouped by date, allowing you to review past task entries anytime.
