About Pipe Matrix: Light the Network

Pipe Matrix: Light the Network is a logic-driven puzzle game focused on building complete systems through careful pipe rotation.

Each level presents a grid filled with pipes and colored bulbs. All pieces are already placed, but the network is broken. Your goal is to rotate the pipes so that every bulb is connected only to bulbs of the same color.

The challenge grows with larger grids, more colors, and complex layouts that require planning and system thinking. One rotation can affect the entire network.
