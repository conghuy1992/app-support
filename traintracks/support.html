About Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle

Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle is a logic-based puzzle game where your goal is to build a single continuous railway track across a grid.

Each puzzle begins with two fixed entrance and exit track pieces placed on the edge of the board. Your task is to place the remaining tracks so that one unbroken railway connects them.

Numbers shown on the top and left of the grid act as clues, indicating how many track pieces must appear in each row and column. Every placement matters.
