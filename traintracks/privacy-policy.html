Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle ("the App") handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping data usage minimal, transparent, and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Local data only: Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle stores game-related data such as puzzle progress, completed levels, hints usage, gameplay statistics, and user preferences locally on your device. This allows the App to be fully playable offline.

No personal information: The App does not request or collect personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, or precise location information.

2. Analytics and Advertising

Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle may use third-party analytics or advertising services (for example, Google AdMob) to improve gameplay experience and support the App through advertisements.

These third-party services may collect non-personal information, such as device type, operating system version, general usage statistics, and anonymous advertising identifiers. We do not directly access, store, or control data collected by these providers.

Users can manage or opt out of personalized advertising through their device’s system settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit personal or gameplay data to external servers.

If future updates introduce online features such as cloud saves, leaderboards, or online challenges, users will be clearly informed, and consent will be requested where required by law.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. Use of the App is at your own discretion.

4. Children’s Privacy

Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle is suitable for players of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If any personal data from a child is identified, it will be removed promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics tools. This Privacy Policy applies only to Train Tracks: Railway Puzzle and does not cover third-party services.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those third-party providers separately.
