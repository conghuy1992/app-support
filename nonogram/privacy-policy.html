Last updated: September 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled by the mobile application Nonogram Nova ("the App"). We respect your privacy and strive to keep any information handling simple and transparent.

1. Information We Collect

Local-only data: Nonogram Nova stores game progress and completed puzzle records locally on your device so you can resume and keep a log of solved puzzles. This includes information such as board size, difficulty level (Intermediate, Hard, Advanced, Expert), and whether a puzzle is completed.

No personal account required: The App does not require registration or user accounts and does not collect personal information (such as name, email, contacts, photos, microphone, or GPS location) for normal gameplay.
