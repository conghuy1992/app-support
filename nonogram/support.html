FAQ

Q: The app freezes or becomes unresponsive?
A: Close the app and reopen it. If the issue continues, restart your device and try again. If it still happens, send us an email with details (device, OS version, and steps to reproduce).

Q: Is Nonogram Nova free?
A: Yes, the app is free to download. It may contain optional ads. There are no required sign-ins to play.

Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes. The core gameplay works completely offline once the app is installed.

Q: Does the game save my progress?
A: Yes. Your current puzzle progress and a record of solved puzzles are saved locally on your device so you can resume at any time.

Q: How do I change difficulty or board size?
A: From the main menu, choose "New Game" and select the desired difficulty (Intermediate, Hard, Advanced, Expert) and board size before starting.

Q: How can I request new puzzles or features?
A: Email us with your feature idea or sample puzzles. We welcome community suggestions and may include popular requests in future updates.
