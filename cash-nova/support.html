📄 FAQ

Q: Can I use Cash Nova offline?
A: Yes. All features work perfectly offline, and no internet connection is required.

Q: Is Cash Nova free?
A: Yes! The app is free to use, with optional ads to support further development.

Q: How can I customize the calendar view or start day of the week?
A: Go to the Settings section in the app to change your calendar view (week, 2 weeks, month) and choose the start day (Monday or Sunday).
